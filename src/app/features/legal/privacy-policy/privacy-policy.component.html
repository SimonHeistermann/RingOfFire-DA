3. Data We Collect

3.1 Game Data (Player Names)

When you create or join a game, player names entered during gameplay are stored in our Firebase Firestore database. These names are freely chosen by the user and may or may not constitute personal data. No registration, email, or account is required.

3.2 Technical Data

When you visit this website, the following data may be automatically collected by our hosting and backend infrastructure:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Date, time, and duration of access
  • Pages visited

3.3 Firebase Services

This application uses Google Firebase for real-time data synchronization (Firestore). Firebase may collect technical metadata such as instance IDs and connection data as part of its service operation. A Firebase measurement ID is configured but no Google Analytics data is actively collected by this application.

9. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR) — Request confirmation of whether we process your personal data and obtain a copy.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR) — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) — Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18 GDPR) — Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR) — Request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR) — Object to processing based on legitimate interests at any time.
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR) — Where processing is based on consent, withdraw at any time without affecting prior lawfulness.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at simon@heistermann-solutions.de. We will respond within one month.
